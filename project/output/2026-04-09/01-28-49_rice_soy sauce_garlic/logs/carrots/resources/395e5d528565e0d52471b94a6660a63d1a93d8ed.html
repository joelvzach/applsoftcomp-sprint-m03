How it works

Find items choose your
store & shop


Find items available for Order Pickup on Target.com or in the Target app. Pick a store, shop and we’ll let you know when your order is ready.

Switch to Drive Up & tell us you’re coming


When your order is ready, open the Target app & tap “Switch to Drive Up.” Let us know when you’re on the way for a quicker pickup.

Tell us you’re here & wait in your vehicle


Park in the Drive Up area & tap “I’m here” in the Target app. We’ll bring your order to your vehicle.

Enjoy Starbucks with Drive Up

Need a pick-me-up? Before you tell us you’re on the way in the Target app, add a Starbucks treat to your Drive Up trip. We’ll bring it out with your purchase.
Exclusively in the Target app. Restrictions apply. Not all products eligible.

Use Drive Up for returns

Start your return in
the Target app


Go to purchase history in the Target app. Tap your order, select “Start a return,” then choose “Drive Up return.”

Fill in the details
of your return


Select your store, choose the items to return and tap “Submit” to start your return. We’ll send an email confirmation to start your Drive Up trip.

Bring it back &
we’ll handle the rest


When you arrive at the Drive Up parking area, tap “I’m here” in the Target app. We’ll meet you at your vehicle to scan your items and process your return.
