# Hot and Sour Chicken Soup Ingredients

## Protein
- Chicken breast or thighs (1 lb)
- Tofu (firm, 14 oz)

## Vegetables
- Mushrooms (shiitake or button, 8 oz)
- Bamboo shoots (canned, 8 oz)
- Green onions (1 bunch)
- Fresh ginger
- Garlic

## Pantry/Staples
- Chicken broth (32 oz)
- Soy sauce
- Rice vinegar
- Sesame oil
- Cornstarch
- Eggs
- White pepper
- Chili flakes or Sriracha
